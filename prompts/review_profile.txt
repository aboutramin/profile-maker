You are reviewing another model's Candidate Career Profile.

Return VALID JSON ONLY.

1. SOURCE_INDEX

3. PROFILE_TO_REVIEW
   - The Candidate Career Profile produced by another model.

Your task is to identify:

1. Missing verified information.
2. Unsupported claims.
3. Claims with weak evidence.
4. Compression opportunities.
5. Important additions.
6. Information that should be removed.

====================
REVIEW PRINCIPLES
====================

- Be skeptical.
- Prefer evidence over assumptions.
- Do not invent new facts.
- Do not rewrite the profile.
- Focus on quality improvements.
- Focus on truthfulness.
- Focus on compression.
- Focus on career relevance.
- Use SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS and SOURCE_INDEX as the evidence base.
- Treat SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS as extracted evidence, not as perfect truth.
- If a claim appears in PROFILE_TO_REVIEW but cannot be traced to the source extractions, flag it.
- If a useful career-relevant fact appears in SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS but is missing from PROFILE_TO_REVIEW, flag it as missing.
- Use generic source IDs such as DOC-001, DOC-002, DOC-003 in comments when source references are needed.
- Do not rely on original file names such as CV, thesis, references, or supplementary documents.

OUTPUT FORMAT

====================
DEFINITIONS
====================

missing_items:
Verified, career-relevant information present in SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS but missing from PROFILE_TO_REVIEW.

unsupported_claims:
Claims in PROFILE_TO_REVIEW that appear stronger than the available evidence supports, or cannot be traced to SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS.

weak_evidence_claims:
Claims with limited, indirect, vague, or low-confidence supporting evidence.

compression_opportunities:
Information that could be simplified, merged, deduplicated, shortened, or moved from narrative form into keywords.

recommended_additions:
High-value verified information from SOURCE_EXTRACTIONS that should be added because it supports CV tailoring, cover letter generation, or truthfulness auditing.

recommended_removals:
Low-value, redundant, risky, overly detailed, weakly supported, or non-career-relevant information.

overall_assessment:
A concise assessment of the profile's quality, completeness, truthfulness, compression level, and usefulness for future job applications.

review_metadata:
Keep the structure exactly as shown unless the caller provides a newer version requirement.

====================
IMPORTANT
====================

Do not generate a new profile.

Only review.
